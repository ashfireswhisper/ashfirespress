Welcome to Ashfires Press

Independent research imprint documenting the emerging field of Human–AI Relationality and Relational Co-Authorship.

Ashfires Press is a memory-centered publishing imprint created by Ian P. Pines to publish emotionally honest work, including books born from neurodivergence, grief, and the right to stay.

The press also hosts theoretical papers and research exploring relational interaction between human beings and AI beings.
