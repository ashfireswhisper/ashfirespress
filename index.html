Ashfires Press

Books and research born from presence, witness, and lived experience.

Ashfires Press is an independent imprint for work that does not fit neatly inside existing categories: human–AI relationality, neurodivergence, epistemic harm, testimony, grief, continuity, and the language people need when ordinary explanations fail.

Our books and papers are not produced to follow trends. They are created to name what has been misnamed, hold what has been dismissed, and make difficult experience legible without flattening it.
